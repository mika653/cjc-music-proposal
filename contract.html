Article 10

Ongoing Maintenance

Applies to Growth and Pro plans only.

Monthly retainer covers the specified number of product updates (price changes, availability, new items, etc.)

The retainer may be cancelled with 15 days written notice before the next billing cycle

Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement with 15 days written notice
If the Client terminates before completion, all work completed will be delivered; payments made are non-refundable but no further payments are required
