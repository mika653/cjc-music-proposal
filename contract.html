Article 1

Parties

Service Provider: Fishbone Creatives (hereinafter "the Provider")

Client: CJC Music (hereinafter "the Client")

Article 2

Scope of Work

The Provider agrees to design and develop a custom website for the Client, including:

Custom responsive website — mobile-first, performance-optimized
Dynamic product catalog across 6 categories: Pro Audio, Instruments, Event Lighting, Studio Gear, LED & AV, DJ Equipment
Authorized dealer showcase section
"Request a Quote" and "Request a Demo" conversion system
Contact forms, Messenger integration, and Google Maps
Basic SEO setup — meta tags, sitemap, Google-friendly structure
Google Analytics integration
Deployment to Vercel or the Client's preferred hosting

Article 4

Client Responsibilities

The Client agrees to provide:

Domain registration (₱800–₱1,500/year via Namecheap, GoDaddy, or preferred registrar)
Hosting account (free via Vercel or paid hosting of choice)
Product photos, descriptions, pricing, and brand assets needed for the website
Timely feedback during the review phase — within 2 business days

The Provider will guide the Client through domain and hosting setup. All accounts remain under the Client's ownership.

Article 5

Timeline

Estimated delivery: 5–7 business days from the date content and assets are received.

Day 1–2: Discovery & Planning
Day 3–5: Design & Development
Day 5–6: Review & Revisions (up to 2 rounds)
Day 7: Launch & Handover

Timeline begins once the initial payment is received and all required content and assets are provided by the Client.

Article 6

Revisions

Up to 2 rounds of revisions are included in all plans
A "revision" covers changes to layout, content placement, colors, or typography
Structural changes — new pages, features, or functionality not in the original scope — will be quoted separately
Additional revision rounds beyond the included 2 will be billed at ₱500 per round
Article 7

Ownership & Intellectual Property

Upon full payment, the Client owns all rights to the website code, design, and content
The Client retains full ownership of their domain, hosting account, and all credentials
The Provider may display the completed project in their portfolio unless otherwise agreed in writing
Third-party assets (fonts, stock images, libraries) remain subject to their respective licenses
Article 8

Ongoing Maintenance

Applies to Growth and Pro plans only.

Monthly retainer covers the specified number of product updates (price changes, availability, new items, etc.)
Unused updates do not roll over to the following month
The retainer may be cancelled with 15 days written notice before the next billing cycle
Upon cancellation, the Client retains full access to and ownership of the website
Article 9

Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement with 15 days written notice
If the Client terminates before completion, all work completed will be delivered; payments made are non-refundable but no further payments are required
If the Provider terminates, all work and assets completed will be delivered to the Client at no additional charge
Article 10

Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep confidential any proprietary information shared during the project, including but not limited to business strategies, pricing, customer data, and technical details. This obligation survives the termination of this agreement.

Article 11

Limitation of Liability

The Provider's total liability shall not exceed the total amount paid by the Client under this agreement
The Provider is not liable for any indirect, incidental, or consequential damages
The Provider is not responsible for downtime caused by third-party services (hosting provider, domain registrar, etc.)
Article 12

General Provisions

This agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties
Any amendments must be made in writing and signed by both parties
This agreement is governed by the laws of the Republic of the Philippines
